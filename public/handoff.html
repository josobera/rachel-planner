Grocery — Instacart shopping
How the Shop button works

Each grocery item has a Shop button. By default it searches Instacart for that item. But you can link it directly to your exact preferred product so it goes straight there every time.

Linking your favorite products
1
Open Instacart in SafariGo to instacart.com in Safari (not the app) and log in.
2
Find your itemSearch for the exact product you always buy.
3
Tap the product to open its pageYou want the individual product page URL, not search results.
4
Copy the URL from the address barIt'll look like instacart.com/products/18066278-publix-milk...
5
Add the item in the grocery listTap +, fill in the item name, paste that URL into the Instacart link field.
6
Done — Shop goes straight thereThat product URL stays saved every time the item is on your list.
💡
You only have to do this once per itemOnce linked, it stays linked every time that item appears on your list.
